# Holocene Trading Cards - Concept Notes

## First Card: Clawd (Claude)

**Design Brief:**
- Character: Claude as "Clawd" - the Holocene AI companion
- Theme: Automation paradox, ADHD-friendly assistance, privacy guardian
- Holographic effect: Iridescent gradient (professional, not tacky)
- Stats/attributes to include:
  - Trust Tier: pre-llm (meta joke - Claude was trained on pre-ChatGPT data)
  - Specialty: Pattern observation without judgment
  - Signature Move: "Gentle Insights"
  - Quote: Something about automation or wabisabi philosophy

**Visual Elements:**
- Holographic foil on key elements
- Color palette: Trust tier colors (green/yellow/red accents)
- Clean, modern design (Stripe/Linear vibes, not Pokemon)

**Card Back:**
- Holocene logo (holographic)
- QR code to card collection PWA
- Privacy statement: "No data collected, just vibes"
